Selected Projects

PSI Racing – Suspension & Steering Subsystem

NIT Trichy • Design, validation, manufacturability

Designed and validated BAJA ATV components that could actually be made in the campus workshop. Modeled in PTC Creo and ran ANSYS Workbench checks for steering, cornering and knuckle loads to stay inside SAE BAJA limits.

Highlights: CAD, FEA, vehicle-level validation

PINN of LMD (Laser Metal Deposition)

PyTorch • Physics-Informed ML • Additive

Built a PINN to predict 3D temperature fields in LMD by enforcing the heat-transfer PDE and realistic laser boundary conditions directly in the loss. Compared against FEM / literature to keep solutions physical.

Code: github.com/gmharish190403/LMD_OPT

Magnetorheological Clutch – Control & Testing

Western University, Canada • Research

Designed and tested a mini MR clutch with a planetary layout and instrumented it with Arduino Mega 2560 to acquire speed/torque. Validated the model with experimental runs.

Optimization of Wire Arc Additive Manufacturing

NIT Trichy • Simufact Welding

Simulated WAAM to study distortion and residual stress across multi-layer beads and tuned process parameters for better stability.

Coaxial UAV Motor Mount (in progress)

Penn UAV Lab • hardware under work

Early design of a mount to connect a brushless motor to a carbon tube in a coaxial stack. Currently working through clamp geometry and fastener access so it can be 3D printed / machined quickly for lab tests.

PINO for WAAM (in progress)

Neural operators • AI for science • course project

Building a Physics-Informed Neural Operator for Wire Arc Additive Manufacturing to solve reverse / parameter-identification problems faster than running full simulations. Part of an AI-for-science style course — still being developed.
