Selected Projects

PSI Racing – Suspension & Steering Subsystem

NIT Trichy • Design, validation, manufacturability

Designed and validated components for the BAJA ATV, focusing on parts we could actually fabricate in the NIT Trichy workshop. Modeled in PTC Creo and ran ANSYS Workbench checks for cornering, steering gear, and knuckle loads to stay within SAE BAJA limits.

Highlights: CAD, FEA, vehicle-level validation, design documentation

Coaxial UAV Motor Mount (in progress)

Penn UAV Lab • CAD (SolidWorks/Creo) • Hardware

Ongoing design of a motor mount to interface a brushless motor with a carbon tube in a coaxial UAV stack. Current iteration focuses on clamp geometry, fastener accessibility, and keeping the part printable/machinable for quick lab tests.

Status: modelling & design iterations underway

PINN of LMD (Laser Metal Deposition)

PyTorch • Physics-Informed Neural Networks • Additive

Built a PINN to predict 3D temperature fields in LMD by enforcing the heat-transfer PDE and realistic laser boundary conditions directly in the loss. Compared the PINN results with FEM / literature to keep the solution physically meaningful.

Code: github.com/gmharish190403/LMD_OPT

Magnetorheological Clutch – Control & Testing

Western University, Canada • Research

Designed and tested a miniaturized planetary MR clutch to get variable torque transfer. Instrumented with Arduino Mega 2560 for speed/torque acquisition and compared the experimental results with the analytical model.

Optimization of Wire Arc Additive Manufacturing

Final-year project • NIT Trichy

Simulated WAAM in Simufact Welding to reduce residual stresses/distortion across multi-layer deposits. Used Goal Programming for process parameter selection and validated with experimental trials.

Tools: Simufact Welding, MATLAB, CAD
